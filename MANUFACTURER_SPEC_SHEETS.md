## GENERAL REQUIREMENTS (ALL STYLES)

### Fabric Quality
- All cotton must be long-staple, combed, ring-spun (minimum 30s count)
- French terry must be 3-end fleece construction (not 2-end)
- GSM tolerance: ±5%
- Pre-shrinkage treatment required (max 3% shrinkage after home wash)
- Oeko-Tex Standard 100 certification minimum
- GOTS organic certification preferred for hero pieces

### Construction Standards
- 4-thread overlock on all internal seams
- Flatlock/coverlock on visible seams
- Bartack reinforcement at all stress points (pocket entry, drawcord exit, cuff join)
- Double-needle bottom hem
- All seams must withstand 30N pull strength minimum

### Hardware
- Metal aglets: Custom matte black finish, laser-engraved "TK" logo
- YKK zippers where applicable
- All hardware must pass nickel-free testing (EU REACH compliant)

### Embroidery Specifications
- Thread: Madeira or equivalent polyester embroidery thread
- Density: 7-8 stitches/mm for dense fill
- Backing: Medium-weight tear-away stabiliser
- Font reference: Helvetica Black / Impact — bold, flat, straight, uppercase
- NO arched text, NO varsity/collegiate style

### Labelling
- Main label: Woven damask, fold-in, back neck centre — "TRUST KARMA" branding
- Care label: Printed satin, side seam — standard UK/EU care symbols + spiritual sarcasm care line
- Size label: Woven, side seam
- Hang tag: Custom designed (to be supplied)

### Packaging
- Individual poly bag (recycled PE)
- Tissue wrap
- Custom branded sticker seal
- Carton box for shipping (max 20 units per carton)

### Testing Required
- GSM verification
- Wash test (5 cycles, 30°C)
- Shrinkage test (<3%)
- Colour fastness (ISO 105-C06, minimum grade 4)
- Pilling resistance (ISO 12945, minimum grade 3-4)
- Seam strength
- Embroidery wash durability (50 cycles)
